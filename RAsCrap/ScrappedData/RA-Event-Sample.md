Washington, D.C. — Get ready to elevate your Halloween night at El Techo, the city’s favorite rooftop oasis, for a one-of-a-kind Halloween Happy Hour celebration. This year’s event fuses deep beats, haunting visuals, and spooky-good cocktails in true El Techo fashion.  Enjoy special performances by Left Locusta, Dommie, Rainier, and Martin Mella, with immersive visual artistry by Timoteo Murphy, transforming the rooftop into a high-vibe haunted paradise.  Come dressed to impress — the best costume of the night wins a bottle of Casamigos and a $50 El Techo gift card.  Sip on exclusive Casamigos cocktail specials crafted just for the night:  Blood Moon Margarita – Tequila Blanco, Citronge, Lime, Watermelon Purée  Nightmare on El Techo St. – Tequila Reposado, Simple Syrup, Mango Purée, Lime, Cava  Trick or Treat Flight – Blanco, Reposado, Añejo  Don’t miss this rooftop Halloween fiesta filled with good vibes, glowing visuals, and signature El Techo energy.
